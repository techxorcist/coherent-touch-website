Coherent Touch Education

Henry teaches workshops and continuing education classes throughout the West, as well as maintaining his private practice as a Licensed Massage Therapist in Missoula, Montana since 1996.

Professional Development Workshop

This two-day workshop is geared towards creating advanced skills for massage therapists and body workers. It would also be applicable for physical therapists and occupational therapists.

What You'll Discover

Increased Self-Awareness

Appreciate your body as a dynamic system that is ever changing

Feldenkrais® Method Introduction

Learn somatic disciplines and movement education techniques

Mindful Movement

Move with qualities of intention and attention in present time

Refined Touch

Experience new ways of engaging gravity and refining your sense of touch

Work Efficiency

Discover how to work with more efficiency and less effort

Career Longevity

Learn techniques to prolong your career as a bodyworker

"My interest in teaching stems from a career of over 27 years as a body worker that has given me so much for so long. It is time for me to give back to my profession and offer a helping hand to my colleagues."

Henry Cloud

A New Perspective

As Marcel Proust said: "True discovery comes not by looking for new landscapes but in looking with new eyes."

This workshop invites you to be curious and playful while discovering new approaches to bodywork that will enhance both your practice and your well-being.
