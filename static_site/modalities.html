Modalities

Henry draws on multiple therapeutic modalities to provide personalized treatment for each client. Each session is tailored to your unique needs, combining techniques from various disciplines to promote healing and well-being.

Therapeutic Massage

Evidence-based massage therapy that addresses specific conditions including anxiety, chronic pain, fibromyalgia, and more. Using professional techniques to release tension and promote the body's natural healing response.

Cranial Sacral

A gentle, hands-on technique that works with the craniosacral system to enhance the body's natural healing processes. Effective for migraines, chronic pain, stress-related disorders, and central nervous system conditions.

Feldenkrais®

A unique approach to movement education that improves flexibility, balance, and posture while reducing pain and dysfunction. Uses curiosity, intention, and mindful movement to unlock your body's potential.

Lymphatic Massage

Gentle, specialized massage that supports the lymphatic system's vital role in immune function and detoxification. Helps reduce swelling, boost immunity, and improve overall health.
