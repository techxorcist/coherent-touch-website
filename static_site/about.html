About Henry

Henry has been a massage therapist since his initial training at the Esalen Institute in 1989. He became certified in The Feldenkrais Method® of movement education after completing a 4-year training in 1992. Since then he has trained in the manual therapies of lymphatic massage, myofascial release, craniosacral therapy, trigger point therapy and Reiki. Henry teaches workshops and continuing education classes throughout the West, as well as maintaining his private practice as a Licensed Massage Therapist in Missoula, Montana since 1996.

Drawing on his areas of expertise Henry tailors each session to the unique needs of individual clients. He has worked on newborns, centenarians, and all ages in between. By bringing focused attention throughout each session, Henry's clients reach a state of relaxation which enhances the body's natural ability to heal and regenerate.

Henry lives in the Bitterroot Valley with his wife Patricia.
