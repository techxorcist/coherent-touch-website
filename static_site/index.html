About Henry

Henry has been a massage therapist since his initial training at the Esalen Institute in 1989. He became certified in The Feldenkrais Method® in 1992. He has trained in the manual therapies of lymphatic massage, myofascial release, craniosacral therapy, trigger point therapy and Reiki.
