The Course

This two-day workshop is geared towards creating advanced skills for massage therapists and body workers. It would also be applicable for physical therapists and occupational therapists.

Course Philosophy

Through this workshop you will increase self-awareness and appreciate your body as a dynamic system that is ever changing. You will have an introduction to the Feldenkrais® Method, as well as other somatic disciplines. You will be moving with the qualities of intention and attention in present time and experience new ways of engaging gravity and refining your sense of touch.

The goal of this course is to discover how to work with more efficiency, less effort and prolong your career.

"My interest in teaching stems from a career of over 27 years as a body worker that has given me so much for so long. It is time for me to give back to my profession and offer a helping hand to my colleagues."

Henry Cloud

Two-Day Workshop Structure

Day One: Inner Work & Self-Discovery

Awareness Through Movement Lessons

You will be experiencing Awareness Through Movement Lessons, doing the inner work on yourself. This is a time to be curious and playful. This is not a time to be judgmental of yourself or others.

What to Bring:

  • Warm, comfortable clothes
  • Yoga mat
  • Blanket

Activities:

You will be moving around the room, lying, rolling and sitting on your mat, as well as sitting in chairs. The focus is on developing new awareness through mindful movement.

"True discovery comes not by looking for new landscapes but in looking with new eyes."

Marcel Proust

Day Two: Applied Skills & Partner Work

Hands-On Practice

Bringing your new skills from day one, you will be exchanging work with your partner. Henry will invite you to wear your "hat of curiosity" as you further fine tune your touch.

What to Bring:

  • Massage table
  • Sheets
  • Blanket

Focus:

When you pay attention to the intelligence of your body as you work on your partner, you will begin to notice the quality of your work will improve. Your partner will notice too.

Course Details

Duration

Each day will be 7 hours which includes a 1 hour lunch break

Target Audience

Massage therapists, bodyworkers, physical therapists, and occupational therapists

Learning Style

Experiential learning through movement, touch, and mindful awareness

This unique workshop combines decades of professional experience with proven somatic education techniques. Discover how to work with greater ease, efficiency, and longevity in your bodywork career.
